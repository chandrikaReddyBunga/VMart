What is Vedas Mart ? Are Vedas Mart Pick Up Points different from Vedas Mart Stores?

   

Vedas Mart is the name of Vedas Mart new online service. Through this service, we hope to bring Vedas Mart closer to you. How? We have opened several Vedas Mart Pick Up Points for your convenience across Mumbai. Unlike our regular Vedas Mart store, Vedas Mart Pick Up Points are specific locations from where you can collect your online order. While placing an order on our mobile app or web site, you can select from a list of pick up points that are closest to you, and choose a time slot that is convenient for you. We will process your order and keep it at your chosen Vedas Mart Pick Up Point. You can collect your order at any time during your time slot - FREE of charge! Now you can buy all your favourite products online from our app or web site, and enjoy Vedas Mart daily discounts and savings.

 

How do I choose a Vedas Mart Pick Up Point?

     

Order Pick Up from Vedas Mart Pick Up Point is completely free of delivery charges. Choosing a convenient Vedas Mart Pick Up Point is very simple.

 

      At the time of selecting your mode of delivery, select "Pick Up Point"

      On selecting the option "Pick Up Point", a list of Vedas Mart Pick Up      Points will be displayed.

      You can also search for a Vedas Mart Pick Up Point by pin code.

      Choose a convenient Pick Up Point from the displayed list.

 

We will keep your order at this chosen Vedas Mart Pick Up Point and timeslot for pick-up.

Yes, we do have a minimum order value. In order to place an order with us, your minimum cart value should be Rs. 1,000/-.

I forgot my password, what do I do?

 

If you don't remember your password and are not able to Sign In, below are the easy steps to reset the password:

1.     Click on 'Forgot Password' link.

2.     Enter your registered mobile number and click on the "Forgot Password" button below. You will receive an OTP for verification purpose.

3.     On 'Account Verification' page, enter the One Time Password (OTP) that was sent to the entered mobile number.

4.     Click "SUBMIT".

5.     You will be redirected to "Change Password" page. Enter your desired new password and click on "UPDATE".

Now you can use the new password to Sign In on your Site/App.

      Your new mobile number is registered which you can now see on your 'My Account' page.
